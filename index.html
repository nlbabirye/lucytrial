About Us

Our mission is to promote sustainable agricultural practices in the Rwenzori Region through innovative research and community engagement. We work closely with local farmers to develop and implement environmentally friendly farming techniques that improve livelihoods and protect biodiversity.

Our Projects

  • **Agroforestry:** Planting trees on farms to enhance soil fertility, improve water retention, and provide shade for crops.
  • **Organic Farming:** Promoting the use of natural fertilizers and pest control methods to reduce chemical pollution and protect the environment.
  • **Community Gardens:** Establishing community gardens to provide fresh, nutritious food and promote local food security.

Research Findings

Our research has shown that agroforestry practices can increase crop yields by up to 20% and reduce soil erosion by 50%. Additionally, organic farming has been found to improve soil health and biodiversity.
